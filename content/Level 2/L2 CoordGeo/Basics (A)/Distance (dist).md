## <u>Distance:</u>
The distance formula comes from the same right angles triangle, except the Pythagoras Theorem is used:

<img src="/images/25686e42d5a68502cbaf559e6763bf35.png" style='width: 500px;' />

$$D^2=(y_{2}-y_{2})^2+(x_{2}-x_{1})^2$$
$$D=\sqrt{ (y_{2}-y_{1})^2+(x_{2}-x_{1})^2 }$$

Using the formula, the distance between two points can be found.
