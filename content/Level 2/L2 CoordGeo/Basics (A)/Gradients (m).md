## <u>Gradients:</u>
The gradient is often measured in $m=\frac{rise}{run}$. However, in this unit, a formula is used:
$$m=\frac{y_{2}-y_{1}}{x_{2}-x_{1}}$$
This formula comes from a right angled triangle:

<img src="../img/e522b706a0f1f506ee4825a968295d10.png" style='width: 500px;' />
Using this right-angled triangle, the gradient of this function can be found.
This is a formula that will even be used in calculus.
