#L2MAEL 

---

Quadratic
$$
\text{vertex form: }y=a(x-p)^{2}+q
$$
$$
\text{intercept form: }y=a(x-p)(x-q)
$$
Cubic
$$
\text{inflection form: }y=a(x-p)^{3}+q
$$
$$
\text{intercept form: }y=a(x-b)(x-c)(x-d)
$$
Square Root
$$
y=a\sqrt{ x-p }+q
$$
Semi-Circle
$$
y=\sqrt{ r^{2}-(x-p)^{2} } \text{ (note: only semi-circles are allowed)}
$$
Hyperbolic
$$
y=\frac{a}{x-p}+q
$$
Logarithmic
$$
y=a\log_{b}(x-p)+q
$$
Exponential
$$
y=a^{x-p}+q
$$
Trigonometric
$$
y=a\sin b(x-c)+d
$$
$$
y=a\cos b(x-c)+d
$$
$$
y=a\tan b(x-c)+d
$$
